## 1. Study title and purpose (example)

This study explores how a brief text-based conversation interface affects your experience and attitudes during participation. The study is **not** intended to treat illness.

## 2. Procedures

If you agree to participate, you will complete: informed consent → eligibility screening → short baseline survey → random assignment → a multi-turn text conversation with a research assistant program → post-survey → closing information. The full session is expected to take about **30–45 minutes** (individual pace may vary).

## 3. Risks and discomfort

- Discussing alcohol and mood may cause brief discomfort.
- The text system **cannot** detect all crisis situations and is **not** continuously monitored by researchers in real time.
- If you feel distressed or have thoughts of harming yourself or others, stop using this program and contact the resources below or local emergency services.

## 4. Confidentiality and data
